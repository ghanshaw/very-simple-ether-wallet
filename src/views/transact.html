How to Use

To use this app, you must have access to the Ethereum blockchain. You can access by blockchain by downloading Geth and downloading or by downloading MetaMask. You do not need ether-- the demo accounts have ether for that purpose--but you will need to sign each transaction with your own Ethereum account.

Depositing/Withdrawing Ether

This wallet features three demonstration accounts. Feel free to use these accounts to move funds in and out of the wallet. You can also send funds directly to this wallet if you wish. Switch to the homepage to track the wallet balance and the transactions as they occur. In order to move funds into the Simple Wallet, users must approve the movement of ether. Therefore, the Deposit for does not allow users to deposit funds from arbitrary ether addresses, only from demo addresses. However, users can withdrawal funds into any abtrary account. Users are permitted to Deposit (move funds into the Simple Wallet) from any demo account and Withdraw (move funds out of Simple Wallet) into either a demo account or any other Ethereum address.
