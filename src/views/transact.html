Demo Accounts

How to Use

To use this app, you must have access to the Ethereum blockchain. You can access the blockchain by downloading MetaMask or Mist. An ethereum account is required to sign transactions. You will also need a small amount of ether to pay for gas. However, the gas required to carry out a transaction is miniscule (much less than 1¢). The demo accounts have funds which you can use transfer funds.

Depositing/Withdrawing Ether

This wallet features three demonstration accounts. Feel free to use these accounts to move funds in and out of the wallet. You can also send funds directly to this wallet if you wish.

You can Deposit (move funds into the Simple Wallet) from any demo account and Withdraw (move funds out of Simple Wallet) into either a demo account or any other Ethereum address. Just choose an account, select an amount, and approve the transaction through MetaMask or Mist.
